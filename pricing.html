Pricing Questions

What Owners Ask Before They Start

01

Is there a setup fee?

No. Setup is included in every plan — no separate onboarding fee like the $3,000 some platforms charge to get started. You’re building on assets you own from day one.

02

What does “you own everything” actually mean?

Your website, domain, Google Business Profile, phone number, customer list, review profiles, and ad accounts are all in your name from day one. If you ever leave us, you keep every asset. Unlike Hibu or Thryv, we don’t hold your website or data hostage to keep you paying.

03

Which plan should I start with?

Most businesses start with Growth because it covers the biggest leaks — getting found (SEO), never missing a call (AI phone), and following up every lead (CRM). But the honest answer is: get a free audit first. We’ll look at your real numbers and tell you which plan fits, and if Starter is all you need right now, we’ll say so.

04

Can I add or remove services later?

Yes. Plans are designed to scale with you. Start with Starter and add SEO, AI phone, and CRM when you’re ready by moving up to Growth — or add POS & Inventory as an add-on to any tier. Move up or down as your needs change.

05

How fast will this pay for itself?

The math is usually straightforward. One recovered missed call, one reactivated past customer, or one saved job a month often covers the plan — and the system saves you dozens. For most local businesses a single AI-captured after-hours job or a handful of recovered leads pays the monthly fee many times over. Your free audit puts real numbers to it for your business.

06

Are there extra costs on top of the monthly rate?

The only pass-through cost is telecom usage — phone numbers and SMS segments billed at cost, standard industry-wide. On Total Presence, your Google Ads budget is separate (it goes to Google, not us) and you set it. No hidden junk fees, no surprise line items. Everything is on the plain-English dashboard.
