What It’s Costing You

The Missed Call Is the Profit Killer

Small businesses answer only about 37.8% of incoming calls. For every ten that ring, six or seven go unanswered — and 85% of the people who hit voicemail never call back. 62% immediately dial a competitor. Every missed call is a permanent loss of that customer’s lifetime value: ~$850 for a dental patient, $275–$1,200 for a plumbing or electrical service call, $400–$450 for an auto ticket, thousands for a law firm.

The best leads hide after hours. 25–40% of home-service calls come outside 8am–5pm; 48% of plumbing emergencies land between 6pm and 8am — and those emergency tickets are worth $450–$600 versus ~$275 for a standard daytime job. A locked door at 6pm makes you invisible to more than half your potential customers. Meanwhile a web form that comes in at 9pm and gets a reply the next afternoon is a lead that already went cold — 82% of people would rather use a chatbot than wait, and only 18% will wait even 15 minutes.
