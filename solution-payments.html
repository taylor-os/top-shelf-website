How Payments & Financing Works for You

Payments & Invoicing

A branded, easy way to bill customers and let them pay you online in a couple of taps.

Why it matters: You finished the job weeks ago and you're still texting 'just checking in' about that invoice, feeling like a nag while the money sits in someone else's account. Meanwhile your own bills are stacking up. Every day an invoice sits unpaid is a day your cash is working for the customer instead of for you.

35%E-invoices get paid about 35% faster than paper invoices, and invoices with an online-pay option are paid up to 4x faster. unimaze.com
You get: You get paid faster, stop chasing, and always know exactly what's owed and what's landed in the bank.

Financing / Buy-Now-Pay-Later

A simple 'pay monthly' option your customers can choose right when they're deciding to say yes.

Why it matters: A customer needs the full repair but their eyes go wide at the number, so they pick the cheap patch job, or walk away entirely. You leave the bigger, better work on the table because the price scared them off. When a customer can't stomach the lump sum, you lose the sale you should have won.

61%One HVAC & plumbing business increased its average HVAC ticket by 61% (and plumbing 34%) after offering consumer financing. buyfin.com
You get: More customers say yes to the full job, your average ticket goes up, and the money hits your account fast.
