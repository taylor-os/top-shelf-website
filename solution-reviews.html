How Reviews & Reputation Works for You

Review Generation & Reputation Management

A steady, automatic system for asking happy customers for reviews and managing your ratings across the web.

Why it matters: You do great work, but your competitor with 200 reviews shows up first and looks more trusted, while your handful of old ratings makes new customers hesitate and scroll past. Your star rating is the first thing a stranger judges you on, and even a fraction of a star swings real revenue.

5-9%A one-star rise in online rating drives a 5% to 9% increase in revenue for independent local businesses. Harvard Business School (Michael Luca), 'Reviews, Reputation, and Revenue: The Case of Yelp.com' — https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=41233
You get: You build a wall of recent five-star proof that wins the customer's trust before you ever pick up the phone.

Video Testimonial Collection

A simple, guided way to capture real customers telling their story on video, then put those clips to work for you.

Why it matters: Your best customers rave about you in person, but that gold vanishes the second they drive off, while a stranger deciding whether to trust you has nothing to go on but a name. People trust a real customer's face and voice far more than any claim you make about yourself, and that trust is what closes the sale.

79%79% of people have watched a video testimonial to learn about a business, and 77% say it helped convince them to buy. Wyzowl — https://wyzowl.com/testimonials-statistics/
You get: You hand every hesitant prospect living proof that you're the safe choice, doing the convincing for you before you ever talk.
