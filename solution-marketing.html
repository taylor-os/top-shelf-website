What It’s Costing You

Reviews Aren’t a “Nice to Have.” They’re a P&L Line.

97% of consumers read reviews before choosing a local business, and they read about 10 of them, spending 13–14 minutes, before they’ll trust you. 57% won’t even consider a business under 4 stars, and the share demanding 4.5+ is climbing. Old, sparse, or negative reviews actively repel the customers actively looking for you — and 74–85% of consumers consider a review older than three months irrelevant, so a pile of stale five-stars doesn’t save you.

The core insight: only ~0.5–1.2% of customers review unprompted, but 69% review when simply asked. Most owners forget to ask, and when they do it’s maybe 5% of customers. Meanwhile only ~5% of businesses respond to reviews while 89% of consumers expect a response. Every one of those gaps is revenue: review signals are ~15–17% of Google’s local ranking weight, and the Local Pack drives 126% more traffic and 93% more calls than positions 4–10. Automation is what closes all of it — at scale, on autopilot.

What We Do

We Build the Flywheel — and Run It for You

Every capability below answers one question: and here’s what that gets you.

Automated Review Requests After Every Job

The moment a job is marked complete, an invoice is paid, or a ticket closes, the system fires a text and email with a one-tap link straight to your Google review form — plus timed follow-ups.

More reviews: you convert the 69% who review when asked, and a 3-touch sequence collects ~4.1x more than a single ask — volume climbs from ~15 to 200+, always fresh. Higher ranking: pushes you into the Map Pack. More money: a 3-to-5-star jump earns ~25% more clicks and ~44% higher conversion per star.

Smart Routing — Catch Unhappy Customers First

The system asks “how did we do?” first. Happy customers route to Google and public sites; unhappy ones route to a private inbox so you fix it before it becomes a public 1-star.

Higher rating: protects the 57% of buyers who skip anything under 4 stars, and avoids the conversion cliff (a 5.0 → 4.0 slide can drop conversions 30–50%). Less headache: a would-be 1-star becomes a service-recovery win, automatically.

Automated, AI-Assisted Review Responses

Every review gets a fast, on-brand reply automatically — no owner writing responses at 9pm.

More money: businesses that reply to 25%+ of reviews average ~35% more revenue, and people spend up to 49% more at businesses that respond. More reviews: starting to respond raises average rating +0.12 stars and brings 12% more reviews over time (HBR). Fully hands-off.

Email + SMS Follow-Up & Win-Back Campaigns

Every lead and past customer enters an automated sequence, and customers who haven’t returned in X days get an automated win-back offer. SMS gets a 98% open rate.

More money: SMS marketing returns $21–$71 per $1 spent; win-back sequences reactivate 12–18% of lapsed customers at 5–7x lower cost than new acquisition. Repeat business: unlocks the 65% of revenue that repeat customers typically drive. Runs on triggers and timers.

Consistent Social + One-Click Promotions

Scheduled posts keep you visible and top-of-mind, and one-click broadcast offers let you fill a slow week on demand — templated, not a daily chore.

More customers: proof-of-life kills the “are they still in business?” doubt. More money: turns “we’re slow this month” into a revenue lever you can pull any week. Less headache: it’s scheduled, not another thing you have to remember.

The flywheel: more jobs → more review requests → more fresh 5-star reviews → higher rating + ranking → more clicks and calls → more jobs. All running without you lifting a finger.
