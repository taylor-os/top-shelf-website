How Online Booking Works for You

Online Booking & Scheduling

A self-serve booking page where customers pick an open slot and schedule themselves, 24/7.

Why it matters: Half your day is spent playing phone tag over appointment times, and the customer who wanted to book at 9pm just gave up because nobody was there to answer. Every booking that has to wait for business hours is a booking you can lose to a competitor who lets people schedule right now.

40%Roughly 40% of online bookings are made after business hours — appointments you'd never capture if a phone call were the only way in. SchedulingKit — https://schedulingkit.com/statistics/online-booking-statistics
You get: You capture jobs around the clock and get hours of your week back from phone tag and back-and-forth.

Speed-to-Lead & Automated Follow-Up

An automatic instant reply to every new lead, plus a follow-up sequence that keeps nudging until they respond.

Why it matters: A ready-to-buy customer fills out your form at 2pm. You're up a ladder and don't see it until 6. By then they've already booked the guy who texted them back in ninety seconds. The first business to respond usually wins the job, and after five minutes your odds of even reaching that lead fall off a cliff.

21xBusinesses that respond to a lead within 5 minutes are 21 times more likely to qualify that lead than those who wait 30 minutes. MIT / InsideSales study (Dr. James Oldroyd), popularized by Harvard Business Review — https://ainora.lt/blog/lead-response-time-statistics-every-study-2026
You get: You catch more customers at the exact moment they're ready to buy, without having to drop your tools every time your phone buzzes.
