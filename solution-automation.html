How AI & Automation Works for You

Custom AI Agents & Automations

Purpose-built AI workers that handle the repetitive, click-heavy tasks in your business so your people don't have to.

Why it matters: You and your team burn hours every week on the same manual busywork — copying data between apps, chasing paperwork, re-typing the same info — and it never ends. The people you pay to grow the business are stuck being data-entry clerks. Every hour spent on manual coordination is an hour you're paying full wages for work a machine could do for free.

11.5 hours/weekEmployees save a median of 11.5 hours per week through automation, while business owners save a median of 5 hours. flowlu.com
You get: Your team gets 10+ hours a week back to actually serve customers and bring in revenue — same payroll, far more output.

AI Knowledge Assistant (trained on your business)

A private AI trained on your own documents, pricing, and policies that answers questions instantly for your team and customers.

Why it matters: Every new hire asks the same twenty questions, and the answers only live in your head or a binder no one can find. You're the bottleneck — the business stops when you're not there to explain how things work. Time you spend re-explaining the same things is time you're not spending growing the company, and wrong answers cost you customers.

3+ hours/day67% of knowledge workers spend 3+ hours a day on manual coordination and information-gathering tasks that automation could eliminate. processmaker.com
You get: New staff get productive faster, customers get instant answers, and you stop being the human help desk for your own company.

AI Outbound Voice Agent

An AI that makes real phone calls for you — following up on leads, confirming appointments, and reaching back out — in a natural voice.

Why it matters: A hot lead fills out your form at 2pm and nobody calls back until tomorrow — by then they've already hired the competitor who picked up first. Every quote you don't chase fast is money walking out the door. Leads go cold in minutes, and the business that responds first almost always wins the job.

78%78% of customers buy from the first company that responds to their inquiry; a 5-minute response yields 21x higher qualification rates than waiting 30 minutes. verse.ai
You get: You catch leads while they're still hot, book more jobs from the same traffic, and stop losing deals to whoever called back faster.

Voice Dictation & Transcription (OpenWhisper)

Private, open-source speech-to-text that transcribes meetings and lets you dictate notes hands-free — and can run on your own machine so sensitive audio never leaves the building.

Why it matters: You finish the day and still have hours of notes to write — charts, files, meeting recaps — done at the kitchen table after everyone's asleep. The paperwork follows you home every single night. That after-hours documentation is unpaid time stolen from your life, and it's the number-one driver of burnout for clinicians and professionals.

1.2 hours/dayPhysicians average 1.2 hours of after-hours "pajama time" on clinic days finishing notes and charts; documentation is the leading cause of physician burnout. ama-assn.org
You get: You get your evenings back, keep sensitive audio private, and turn hours of typing into minutes of review.

AI Document Extraction

AI that reads your invoices, receipts, and forms and pulls the data straight into your systems — no manual typing.

Why it matters: Someone on your team spends hours hand-keying numbers off invoices and receipts, and one fat-fingered figure throws off your books or overpays a bill. It's mind-numbing work that's also quietly expensive when it goes wrong. Manual data entry is slow, costly per document, and every error costs real money to catch and fix.

$15.97 per invoiceThe average cost to manually process a single invoice is $15.97, and manual entry carries a typical error rate of 1-4%. resolvepay.com
You get: You cut the typing to near zero, kill costly errors, and free your team from the most tedious job in the office.
