Where the Money Is Going

Five Leaks That Are Bleeding You Dry

These aren't small inefficiencies. On a 3–5% margin, any one of these can wipe out an entire year of profit. Most owners are dealing with all five at once.

🥃
Liquor Shrinkage
$18,000–$24,000/year
The average bar loses 20–30% of its inventory value to over-pouring, spillage, and theft. A bar with $10,000/month in product costs at 15% shrinkage is hemorrhaging $1,500 every month — $18,000 a year — before a single customer walks in. Every 1% of shrinkage wipes out 2.5% of your already razor-thin profit. Your bartenders are pouring 15% more alcohol than you're charging for, and your current POS has no idea.
🪑
No-Shows & Last-Minute Cancellations
$39,000–$78,000/year
10–20% of your reservations don't show. A single no-show costs $28–$120 per cover — lost food prep, staffed tables, and a seat that couldn't be given to a walk-in. On 100 weekly reservations at 10% no-shows, you're looking at $39,000–$78,000 in lost revenue annually. Peanut Park Trattoria lost 52 covers on Valentine's Day — roughly $3,000 in a single night — to no-shows with no recourse.
📱
Delivery App Commission Tax
30–40% of every delivery order
DoorDash, Grubhub, and Uber Eats are marketing platforms that charge restaurant prices. Their effective commission — after processing fees, promotional requirements, and refunds — routinely hits 30–40% per order. On a restaurant already running 10–15% margins, that eliminates every dollar of profit on delivery. Worse, you don't own the customer. They're DoorDash's customer. You cooked the food and got nothing.
🔍
Invisible on Google
64% of diners Google before they visit
91% of diners avoid restaurants under 4 stars. 73% only trust reviews written in the last 30 days. "Near me" restaurant searches have surged 875%. If your Google Business Profile is thin, your photos are old, and your reviews haven't been updated since 2023 — you are functionally invisible to the customers actively looking for you right now. A competitor with 4.7 stars and fresh reviews is taking your reservation.
📭
No System to Bring Customers Back
Repeat guests = 60% of revenue — and you can't reach them
Most independent bars and restaurants have no loyalty system, no email or SMS list, and no way to fill a slow Tuesday without offering a blanket discount that kills your margin. Your regulars want to come back — they just forgot. You have no way to remind them. Meanwhile, you're spending on Instagram ads to reach strangers when your best customers' phone numbers are sitting in a POS you can't export.

The Fix

Five Systems. One Stack. Measurable Results.

We don't sell software. We solve the specific problem each leak is causing and show you the math before we start.

Modern POS + Real-Time Liquor Tracking
We connect your POS to pour-cost tracking and automated inventory reconciliation. Every bottle is counted against every drink sold — in real time.
Cut shrinkage from 20–30% to single digits. Recover $1,000–$1,500/month. Real-time alerts flag over-pours and missing bottles before they become a trend. Your 2 a.m. bottle count becomes a 5-minute exception report.

Reservation System + Automated Reminders + Card Holds
Automated multi-touch reminders (SMS + email) go out 48 hours and 2 hours before the reservation. Credit card holds deter habitual no-shows.
Cut no-shows 30%+ on confirmed reservations. On a busy Friday of 80 covers, that's $450–$900 recovered — per night. One Valentine's Day is now protected.

Branded Online Ordering (Commission-Free)
Your own ordering page — your branding, your domain, your customers. We build and integrate it with your kitchen workflow so there's zero friction for your staff.
Keep the 25–35% you were handing to DoorDash on direct orders. You own every customer's name, email, and order history — so you can market to them next time without paying a middleman.

Google Business Profile Optimization + Review Automation
Full GBP audit and rebuild: photos, hours, attributes, posts. Automated post-visit review requests sent via SMS — to the right customers at the right moment.
Every 1-star increase in rating drives a 5–9% revenue lift. Crossing 4.5 stars puts you in the Google local pack, where 875% more searchers are looking. Fresh reviews mean 73% of diners actually trust what they read.
💬
CRM + Loyalty Program + Slow-Night SMS Campaigns
We pull your customer data out of your POS and turn it into a real contact list. Loyalty programs, birthday offers, and targeted SMS campaigns to your own audience — not strangers.
Fill slow Tuesdays with guests who already love you — without a blanket discount. Loyalty members spend 12–18% more per visit. A Tuesday SMS to 1,200 regulars with a specific offer costs pennies and fills tables within hours.
