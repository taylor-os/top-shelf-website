Yes. It answers the call, collects the info, checks your calendar, and books the appointment — just like a receptionist. It handles common questions (“are you open Saturday,” “how much for a water heater”) and routes anything complex to a human. You don’t need to touch it.

Anything the AI can’t resolve gets flagged for immediate callback by your team, with a full transcript of the conversation. You never lose context on a customer — even the ones who needed a human.
